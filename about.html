About me

UX designer, researcher, & strategist based in Washington D.C.

I am an anthropologist-turned-UX designer and strategist who leverages ethnographic methods and behavioral insights to create user-centered, impactful products and services and influence big-picture business strategies.

As a trained anthropologist who grew into UX design, research, and strategy, I bring a deep understanding of human behavior and culture to crafting intuitive, user-centered products. I bridge design and business with a strong knack for product management and strategic thinking. My work consistently delivers solutions that meet real user needs and stakeholder goals.

My graduate research explored the relationship between humans and their environments, with a special interest in how physical and cultural space is created and managed to influence persons and support particular ways of life.
